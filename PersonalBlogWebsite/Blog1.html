Spectacular Waterfalls

Here is a quote from National Geographic website:
A waterfall is a river or other body of water's steep fall over a rocky ledge into a plunge pool below. Waterfalls are also called cascades. The process of erosion, the wearing away of earth, plays an important part in the formation of waterfalls.

What is a waterfall?

Waterfalls are also known as cascades.These are basically formed when water falls from a higher place like hills or mountains to a lower place. Usually,it's a part of a flowing river or stream.

Are waterfalls associated with paradise?

Well, experientially the feel of it is nothing less that a paradise. There's no better place to find yourself than sitting by a waterfall and listening to its music. The freshness of water imbibes in you a sense of living evergy.It almost feels like you are in a paradise far away from the busy urban life. Scientifically, waterfalls are a natural outcome of the errosion process.
