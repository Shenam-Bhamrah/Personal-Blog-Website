MEET THE AUTHOR

Shenam Bhamrah

Shenam Bhamrah is an educator turned blogger. Her love for nature has always been an intrinsic motivator to start her journey as a blogger. A biophilic at heart and a ponderer by nature, she has always been passionate about explaining more and more about the environment. Her mission is to help people connect with nature and become more planet-conscious. Her blogs are an intelligent blend of the science behind natural occurrences as well as the artistic aspect of it. Her blogs portray different elements of nature beautifully. Through her blogs, she aims to create a biophilic community that educates people and spreads the message of being planet-conscious. She would love to hear what the readers think and feel about her blogs.
